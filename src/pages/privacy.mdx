Snorre.io is a personal website and my goal is to track as little as possible about you.

## Cloudflare

The site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages and they collect some information.
You can read more about what they collect in their [privacy policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/) under "End users:".
In summary your IP address and some information about your browser is collected for a limited time.

## Comments

My comment system is powered by the [ATProtocol](https://atproto.com/) and the [Bluesky network](https://blueskyweb.xyz).
When you interact with the comment system all interaction go directly to the Bluesky network.
No information is collected by Snorre.io.
You can check out the [source code](https://github.com/snorremd/snorreio/blob/main/src/components/comments) for the comment system to verify.
Read more about what information is collected by the Bluesky network in their [privacy policy](https://blueskyweb.xyz/support/privacy-policy).
Bluesky is a Public Benefit LLC and will hopefully not resort to selling user data to ad companies.

## Analytics

No analytics are currenetly used on Snorre.io.
